## SYNOPSIS

Retrieves sharing partners.

## SYNTAX

```powershell
Get-IMPartner [[-Session] <ImmichSession>] [-Direction] <String> [-ProgressAction <ActionPreference>]
 [<CommonParameters>]
```

## DESCRIPTION

Gets users who share assets with you or users you share with.

## EXAMPLES

### EXAMPLE 1

